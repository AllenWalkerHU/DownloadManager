Off means download bar is disabled.

Download Completion Notification

On means notifications are enabled when download completes

On Notification Click, Do

select what u want to do when u click on notification

Options

Clear Failed

This Option will clear all downloads that eighter failed due to network error or cancelled by the user

Clear Deleted

This Option will clear all downloads whom files have been deleted form disk

Clear Completed

This Option will clear all downloads which are successfully completed (be it on the disk or not). This doesn't clear cancelled downloads

Note======> This Extension has noting to do with your download speed. People keep on uninstalling this extension (i wonder if they think this is the reason). It does not make your download slow or fast(no one can). Chrome downloads your files, this extension only shows the information (speed, how much downloaded, etc.) (if u don't believe try disabling and enabling it during download, trust me it won't drop your download). If u are using some other extension or software to boost your speed, this extension does not interfere.
